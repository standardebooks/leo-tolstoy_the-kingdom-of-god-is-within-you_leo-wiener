The Kingdom of God Is Within You
was written in 1893 by
Count Lev Nikolayevich Tolstoy.
It was translated from Russian in 1905 by
Leo Wiener.

This ebook was transcribed, edited, and produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
David M. Gross,
with additional work by
Alex Cabal,
and is based on digital scans available at
Google Books.

The cover page is adapted from
The Voyage of Life: Youth,
a painting completed in 1842 by
Thomas Cole.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
